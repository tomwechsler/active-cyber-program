# ACP Assessment and Certification Agreement

## 1. Parties

This agreement is entered into between:

**Wechsler Information Solution**
(hereinafter referred to as "Assessment Authority")

and

**[Organization Name]**
(hereinafter referred to as "Client")

---

## 2. Purpose of the Agreement

The purpose of this agreement is the execution of an **Active Cyber Program (ACP) Assessment** in order to evaluate whether the Client operates an active cybersecurity program in accordance with the **ACP Framework**.

---

## 3. Scope of the Assessment

The assessment will evaluate relevant aspects of the Client’s cybersecurity program, including but not limited to:

• Governance and security strategy  
• Risk management  
• Security policies and processes  
• Identity and access management  
• Infrastructure security  
• Endpoint security  
• Network security  
• Data protection and encryption  
• Monitoring and incident response  
• Continuous improvement  

The exact scope of the assessment will be defined prior to the start of the engagement.

---

## 4. Assessment Methodology

The ACP Assessment may include:

• interviews with relevant personnel  
• review of documentation and policies  
• technical validation of security measures  
• evaluation of monitoring and incident response processes  

The assessment is conducted based on the **ACP Framework and methodology**.

---

## 5. Certification Decision

Following completion of the assessment, the Assessment Authority will determine whether the Client meets the requirements for **ACP Certification**.

If the requirements are met, the Client may receive an **ACP Certification and Trust Label**.

The final certification decision lies solely with **Wechsler Information Solution**.

---

## 6. Use of ACP Certification

Organizations that successfully complete the assessment may use the **ACP Trust Label** in accordance with the official ACP usage guidelines.

Certification validity is typically **24 months**, unless otherwise stated.

---

## 7. Confidentiality

All information obtained during the assessment will be treated as confidential and used solely for the purpose of conducting the ACP assessment.

---

## 8. Limitation of Liability

The ACP assessment represents an evaluation of cybersecurity practices at a specific point in time.

The assessment does not guarantee the absence of vulnerabilities or security incidents.

---

## 9. Fees

Fees for the assessment will be defined in a separate commercial agreement or proposal.

---

## 10. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement prior to the start of the assessment with written notice.

---

## 11. Governing Law

This agreement shall be governed by the applicable laws of the jurisdiction of **Wechsler Information Solution**.

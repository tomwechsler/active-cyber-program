# ACP Certification Registry

This registry lists organizations that have received **Active Cyber Program (ACP) Certification**.

The registry provides transparency regarding organizations that have successfully demonstrated an **active cybersecurity program** according to the ACP framework.

Only certifications issued by **Wechsler Information Solution** are considered valid ACP certifications.

# Certification Validity

ACP certifications are valid for **24 months** from the certification date.

Organizations may undergo reassessment to maintain certification.
